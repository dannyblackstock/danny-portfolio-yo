This project was developed as a final project assignment for an Information Design course.

My team of three, including me, developed a prototype app for Blue Mountain Park in Coquitlam, to allow users to find information about and be guided around the various locations of the park.

We studied the how people used the space and facilities there, and how they navigated the park. I worked with my team to create user personas in Illustrator that assisted us with designing for our intended audience.

I designed the overall interface for the app, and around half of the icons for the locations. The interface was designed through sketching and user testing, and then the icons and interface were both finished in Illustrator. I then brought the Illustrator drawings into Flash, and created an interactive demo application.
